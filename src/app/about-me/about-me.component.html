Background

From a young age, I exhibited a keen interest in computers that set me on a path of technological exploration. Growing up, I was the go-to person in my family and circle of friends for troubleshooting computer issues. My fascination with the inner workings of these machines was evident as I eagerly delved into programming and coding, teaching myself the basics of various programming languages.

As I entered college, my passion for computers and software development only intensified. I pursued a degree in computer science and found myself immersed in a world of algorithms, data structures, and software engineering. The process of creating software, whether it was a simple program or a complex application, became a source of joy and fulfillment.

My experiences in college not only solidified my love for software development but also opened doors to exciting opportunities, and collaborations with like-minded individuals who shared the passion for technology. With each line of code I wrote, I knew I was forging a path towards a promising career in the world of software development, where my childhood fascination had grown into a professional calling.

What Do I Do For Fun?

In my cherished moments of leisure, I prioritize an active and diverse lifestyle. I enjoy playing both basketball and softball, relishing the physical exertion and competition they bring.
